Top 7 - Chic 'N' Stu

About This Song

"Chic 'n' Stu" or Pizza Pie is the opening track from the record titled Steal This Album! by System of a Down which came out in 2002. It is a reference to sports broadcasters Chick Hearn and Stu Lantz, and the album version opens with a famous quote often uttered by Hearn when a game's outcome was no longer in doubt. The concept for the song was conceived while watching a Los Angeles Lakers game.
