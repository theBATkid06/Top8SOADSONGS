Top 8 - Suite-Pee

About This Song

Suite-Pee is the first track on System of a Down's self-titled album. The lyrics were written by Serj Tankian, and the music by Daron Malakian.

The lyrics are about religious fanaticism, which are mostly noted by the lyrics "Lie naked on the floor and let the messiah go through our souls" Click to read more on System Of A Down Wiki.
